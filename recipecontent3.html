If you're looking for a filling meal that will satisfy everyone at your table, you've come to the right place. The Allrecipes community loves this top-rated stuffed pepper recipe — reviewers say it's "delicious," "easy," and "full of flavor."
Timing: 1Hr 20min

    Ingredients:
  • For the cake:
  • 4 oz (113g) semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup (40g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (60g) powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
    • For greasing:
  • Butter and cocoa powder (or flour)

    Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place a baking sheet on the middle rack.
  2. Prepare Ramekins: Butter the insides of two 6-ounce (175ml) ramekins or small oven-safe dishes. Dust them with cocoa powder (or flour), shaking out any excess. This will help the cakes release easily after baking.
  3. Melt Chocolate and Butter: In a microwave-safe bowl or using a double boiler, melt the chopped chocolate and butter together until smooth. If using a microwave, heat in 20-second increments, stirring between each until fully melted.
  4. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, sift together the powdered sugar and all-purpose flour. This helps ensure there are no lumps in the final cake.
  5. Mix Eggs and Yolks:In a third bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  6. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the melted chocolate-butter mixture into the egg mixture and stir until well combined.
  7. Bake: Bake for about 12-14 minutes, or until the edges of the cakes are set, but the centers are still slightly jiggly.
  8. Serve: Carefully remove the ramekins from the oven using oven mitts. Let them cool for a minute or two. You can serve the cakes directly in the ramekins or gently run a knife around the edges and invert them onto plates. The lava cakes should come out easily.
  9. Garnish and Serve: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve the stuffed peppers hot, optionally with a side of tomato sauce or sour cream.
    10. Enjoy your delicious homemadeChocolate lava cake!
